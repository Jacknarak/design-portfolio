About

I’m an emerging surface designer creating contemporary, market-ready prints for apparel, home, and stationery. My work focuses on florals, botanicals, soft geometrics, and textured abstracts—built as clean, production-ready repeats with balanced scale and refined color stories. I design with usability in mind: cohesive collections, clear organization, and optional alternative colorways. My goal is to keep learning, build a sustainable creative practice, and feel proud when my artwork lives on real products. I’m open to licensing, collaborations, and custom briefs.
